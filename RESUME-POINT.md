### How to resume Group 3 (or any group) — the actual method that works
1. Get the group's real member list: `git -C ~/flwork-nycmadebigmove log --oneline --reverse <start-sha>..<end-sha>` for the catalog's named range, then sanity-check with `--grep` on the group's real theme keyword — membership is NOT 1:1 with the range.

3. Cherry-pick one at a time: `git cherry-pick -x <sha>`. On conflict, read both sides in full before resolving — this session hit three real shapes, all found this way, not guessed:
